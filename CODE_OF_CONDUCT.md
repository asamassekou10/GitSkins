# Code of Conduct

GitSkins should be a respectful, practical place to build developer tools together.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, direct, and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections quickly.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles.
- Credit people for their work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Discriminatory comments or exclusionary behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, discussions, or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

To report a concern, email gitskinspro@gmail.com.
